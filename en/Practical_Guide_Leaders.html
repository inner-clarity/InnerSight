    # Practical Guide: Common Scenarios for Leaders

    # Scenario 1: A member reveals an unusual regulation need

    ✓ Listen without interrupting or judging ✓ Ask: "How can I best support you?" ✓ If you don't understand, it's okay to say: "This is new to me, could you help me understand?" ✓ Never demand "immediate overcoming" ✓ Consult with mental health professionals if there are doubts

    # Scenario 2: Another member expresses concern

    ✓ Gently educate about neurological diversity ✓ Remind principles of "not judging" ✓ Protect the privacy of the affected member ✓ Encourage empathy and understanding

    # Scenario 3: Situations in Church activities

    ✓ Allow discrete adaptations when possible ✓ Create safe spaces (e.g.: quiet rooms) ✓ Don't publicly point out differences ✓ Normalize diversity in talks/lessons
